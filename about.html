Our Inspiration

Mr Gary Brown is a serial entrepreneur who started building IT-based businesses more than two decades back. As his understanding of technology and the opportunity to provide solutions grew, Mr. Brown looked at the massive gaps in the supply chain platform and saw it as a great opportunity to leverage technology and help move products from point A to point B. EBR started as a side business with an interest in driving efficiency and over time, has now become a very successful logistics and brokerage business. Today, ALG is a tech-driven logistics platform moving millions of dollars of products every month and is quickly becoming a leader in the logistics brokerage space.
