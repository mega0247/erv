Sneak Peek into our OTR Service

1. Full Truckload
ALG has a vast network of professional carriers all over the US with advanced equipment. Our experts know how to handle seasonal and peak sessions with utmost precision. With EBR’s network, passion, and competitive pricing, your businesses can confidently ship your products countrywide.

When should you use FTL?
You should consider FTL if you:

  • Have shipments comprising 10+ pallets
  • These pallets weigh at least 15,000-20,000 lbs, or those filling most or all of a trailer
  • Want to dispatch a full trailer’s worth — 53 feet — of cargo
  • Are on a time-sensitive delivery

2. Less than Truckload (LTL)
EBR has the reputation of providing one of the highest-rated LTL transportation services in the US. Whether you need expedited freight shipping, local shipping, or an international solution, we help you choose the right option to meet your needs.

When should you use LTL?
  • Your business has freight to move, but you don’t necessarily ship enough products to warrant a full truckload.
  • It’s ideal for small- to medium-sized businesses that want shipping efficiency.
